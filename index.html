Mercedes Benz (US) (Sept 2017 - ongoing)

Sales Product and Technology

  • Problem: Abundance of content and a rich video library resulting in a high time investment to learn about Products and Technology
  • Solution: Use a micro-learning approach to create 11 product and technology Lessons of 6-11 min each. Used a gamified approach to keep engagement high

Remote Diagnostic Testing (US)

  • Problem: Non adoption of Remote Diagnostic Testing (RDT) workflow
  • Solution: Use Experiential learning in a virtual dealership. The learner will do a virtual walkthrough of the dealership and ‘e-meet’ the staff and learn about their role, responsibilities as applied to the RDT process and their benefits. Questions thrown at learner promote the emphasis of team work and collaboration amongst the dealership staff and the use of RDT as a tool for demonstrating the MBUSA customer value proposition.
